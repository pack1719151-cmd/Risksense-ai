# Compliance Notes

Document compliance-related notes here.
## Key Compliance Considerations

- **Data Privacy:** Ensure all personal and sensitive data is handled according to GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations.
- **Access Controls:** Implement role-based access and regularly review permissions to maintain least privilege.
- **Audit Trails:** Maintain comprehensive logs for all critical actions to support traceability and accountability.
- **Incident Response:** Establish and document procedures for identifying, reporting, and responding to compliance breaches.
- **Training:** Provide regular compliance training for all team members to stay updated on policies and best practices.
- **Third-Party Risk:** Assess and monitor compliance of vendors and partners with organizational standards.
