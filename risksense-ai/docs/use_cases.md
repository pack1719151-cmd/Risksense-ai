# Use Cases

Document use cases for RiskSense AI here.
## 1. Threat Detection and Prioritization

RiskSense AI analyzes security events and vulnerabilities, prioritizing threats based on risk impact and business context.

## 2. Automated Remediation Guidance

Provides actionable remediation steps for identified risks, streamlining incident response and reducing manual effort.

## 3. Attack Surface Management

Continuously monitors assets to identify exposure points, helping organizations proactively reduce their attack surface.

## 4. Compliance Reporting

Generates compliance reports aligned with industry standards (e.g., PCI DSS, HIPAA), simplifying audit preparation.

## 5. Vulnerability Management

Aggregates vulnerability data from multiple sources, enabling efficient tracking, assessment, and resolution.

## 6. Security Posture Assessment

Evaluates overall security maturity, highlighting gaps and recommending improvements for risk reduction.

## 7. Executive Dashboards

Delivers real-time risk insights and trends to leadership, supporting informed decision-making and resource allocation.
